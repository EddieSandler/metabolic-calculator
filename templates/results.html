How to use this

Turn targets into simple daily actions.

Don’t chase perfection. Use these numbers to anchor your plan: consistent protein, roughly on-target calories, and enough carbs and fats to feel human while you change.

Quick start checklist
  • Hit your protein most days. That alone changes hunger and body composition.
  • Stay within ±10% of your daily calories on average over the week.
  • Pick 2–3 “anchor meals” you can repeat to remove decision fatigue.
What we’ll watch over time
  • Scale trend, measurements, and how your clothes fit.
  • Energy, sleep, and training performance — not just pounds.
  • Whether we need to adjust calories or macros to keep progress moving.
